who am i

manifesto

I am not a resume. I am not a LinkedIn profile. I am not the sum of my achievements or failures.

I am the person who stays up too late thinking about the meaning of existence. I am the one who finds beauty in the cracks of the sidewalk and poetry in the mundane. I am raw, unfiltered, sometimes messy, always real.

This space is not for recruiters or networking. This is for kindred spirits who understand that authenticity is the rarest currency in our hyper-curated world.

I believe in the power of vulnerability. I believe in showing up as your true self, even when it's uncomfortable. I believe that our imperfections make us human, and our humanity makes us beautiful.

If you've stumbled here, maybe you're looking for something real too. Welcome.
